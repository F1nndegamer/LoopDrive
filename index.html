How It Works

LoopDrive is a custom-made ring embedded with real USB flash storage. When placed into the included dock, it acts just like a regular USB stick — fast, secure, and portable.

  • Fully functional USB flash drive (4GB–64GB)
  • Custom circular connector dock
  • Stylish, durable, and wearable
