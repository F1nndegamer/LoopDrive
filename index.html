LoopDrive

A revolution in portable storage – on your finger.

Ultra-portable. Always with you.

LoopDrive is a USB drive built into a wearable ring. No more searching for flash drives — it's right there when you need it.

[Concept Image Placeholder]

Features

  • 🧲 Magnetic Dock with USB Adapter
  • 💡 Always-accessible, worn like a ring
  • 🔒 Secure fit with snap-lock design
  • ⚙️ Swappable internal drive module (e.g., SanDisk Ultra Fit)
  • 📏 Custom circular contact design (4-pin: D+, D-, VCC, GND)

Technical Specifications (WIP)

  • Storage: 16GB / 32GB / 64GB modules supported
  • Connector: 4-contact ring, USB 2.0 compliant
  • Materials: Aluminum, Resin (3D printed)
  • Form Factor: Circular Ring w/ Adapter Dock

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will it work with any USB port?

A: Yes, once docked into its adapter, it's a standard USB-A device.

Q: Can I swap out the storage?

A: The internal flash module is replaceable — more sizes coming soon.

Q: Will there be color options?

A: Likely! We’re testing different filament materials.
